About PIPI

The Platform for Innovation and Productive Ideas (PIPI) is a dedicated organization focused on fostering creativity, collaboration, and the transformation of ideas into actionable outcomes. PIPI operates as an energy research-driven initiative, delivering workable solutions to societal, humanitarian, and environmental challenges.

Vision: To end energy poverty across Africa using Science and Technology as a tool.

Mission: To create an inclusive environment where individuals, teams, and communities can generate, share, refine, and implement innovative ideas that drive progress and efficiency.

Our Objectives

Idea Generation

Encouraging open ideation and brainstorming to spark novel and impactful solutions.

Productive Innovation

Transforming ideas into tangible results such as new products, services, and process improvements.

Collaboration

Facilitating collaboration among innovators, entrepreneurs, experts, and organizations.

Resources & Guidance

Providing tools, mentorship, and guidance for evaluating, validating, and executing ideas.

Activities & Features

  • Idea challenges, technical paper contests, workshops, and conferences
  • Digital platform for submitting, discussing, and voting on ideas
  • Research support and end-to-end innovation management
  • Focus on both incremental improvements and breakthrough innovations
